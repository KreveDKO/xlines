Keep three nails so that the big circle moved into one of the colored ring

HOW TO PLAY: left click or touch on small dots to remove it. When you remove a point, the big circle will move. When you keep three points the big circle must be in one of colored rings. Green, orange, red circle will give you 150, 100, 50 score points and 2, 1, 0 lifes.
