Xlines

Keep three nails so that big circle moved into one of colorful circle

HOW TO PLAY: left click or touch on small dots to remove it. When you remove a point, a big circle will move. When you keep three points a large circle must be in one of colorful circles. Green, orange, red circle will give you respectively 150, 100, 50 score points and 2, 1, 0 lifes.
